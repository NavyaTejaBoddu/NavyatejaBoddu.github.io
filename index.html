About me

Reliability is a system, not a hope.

I'm a DevOps engineer with six years of hands-on experience building and running CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes clusters, and cloud infrastructure on AWS and Azure. Most of that work has been inside HIPAA and PCI-DSS regulated environments for healthcare and banking enterprises, where a missed control or a slow rollback isn't an inconvenience — it's an audit finding.

My approach is to replace anything manual or ad-hoc with something codified, repeatable, and observable: reusable Terraform modules instead of console clicks, Helm-deployed services with autoscaling instead of hand-tuned servers, and SLO dashboards with paging that catch problems before users do.

The throughline across my roles is measurable improvement — cutting deployment time by 45%, dropping mean time to detection from 15 minutes to under two, and holding payment workloads at 99.95% uptime.
